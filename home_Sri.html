Who created me?

The Bible says, God created this whole world with power of his Word. He said let there be light and there was light. In the new Testament, the gospel according to John starts out with the words, in the beginning. The first few lines of the Gospel of John says that this person called the Word, existed from the beginning and he was with God and was God. What a striking correlation between two books, written by two different authors written thousands of years apart from each other. I have only one word to say, WOW!
