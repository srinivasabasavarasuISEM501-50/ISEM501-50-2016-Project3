The Holy Spirit

The Holy Spirit is the third person in the Trinity. He is fully God. He is eternal, omniscient, omnipresent, has a will, and can speak. He is alive. He is a person. He is not particularly visible in the Bible because His ministry is to bear witness of Jesus (John 15:26). A primary role of the Holy Spirit is that He bears "witness" of Jesus Christ.7 He tells people's hearts about the truth of Jesus Christ. The Holy Spirit also acts as a Christian's teacher.8 He reveals God's will and God's truth to a Christian.
